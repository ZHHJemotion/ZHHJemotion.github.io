Hangjian Zhang

I am currently an algorithm engineer in Volkswagen Group China. My research interests lie in video-based computer vision and deep learning, especially video object segmentation, image segmentation, object tracking and efficient model design.

Prior to join Volkswagen Group China, I received my M.Sc. in electrical engineering from Technische Universität Braunschweig in Germany in 2018, advised by Prof. Marcus Magnor. Before coming to TU Braunschweig, I received my B.Eng. in Information Security from Xidian University in China in 2014. During summer 2018, I interned at Intel Labs China as a research intern, advised by Dr. Yimin Zhang and Dr. Haibing Ren .
